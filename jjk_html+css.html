Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese anime and manga series that follows Yuji Itadori as he battles curses with the help of his friends and mentors.

The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and dark themes. The story revolves around Yuji Itadori, a 1st-year high school student with exceptional physical abilities. After the death of his grandfather, Yuji finds himself drawn into the world of Jujutsu Sorcery when he comes into contact with a cursed object—a rotting finger imbued with an ancient curse.

Strongest Sorcerers

Top-tier sorcerers with exceptional cursed techniques.

Our very own Yuji is now all grown up (too much, actually), and in Volume 3 of Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo (via @Go_Jover), he has finally been revealed as a special grade sorcerer.
He is no longer the neglected main character that many claimed he was; he is now the face of the franchise, the strongest in the verse. If you thought Gojo was overpowered, wait until you learn about Yuji’s current abilities. Drastically slowed down aging, Blood Manipulation (controlling blood cells and O2), Sukuna’s techniques, RCT, Black Flash, Domain Expansion, immense strength and speed; you name it, Yuji has it.
His growth after the final battle against Sukuna has been unprecedented over the years, and it was well demonstrated against the rematch against Mahito in Modulo Chapter 22.

Satoru Gojo is a Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest sorcerer in the world. He is the pride of the Gojo Family, the 1st person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years.
He works as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High and uses his influence to protect and train strong young allies.
His Domain Expansion, Unlimited Void, overwhelms the target's mind with endless information, leaving them completely paralyzed.

Yuta Okkotsu is a Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerer who was initially cursed by his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto. After enrolling in Jujutsu High, he learned to channel her immense power.
He possesses even more cursed energy than Satoru Gojo and has the rare ability to unconditionally copy other cursed techniques.
He is a prodigy in combat and is widely considered to be second only to Satoru Gojo in modern sorcery.

Kenjaku is an ancient and powerful curse user in Jujutsu Kaisen who extends his life by transplanting his brain into other bodies, manipulating events over centuries to achieve his goals. He has survived for over a thousand years by using his innate technique, Brain Transplant.
Kenjaku is manipulative, egotistical, and highly intelligent. His ultimate goal is to push human evolution to the next stage.

Yuki Tsukumo is one of the four Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerers. Unlike the others, she does not take on missions from Jujutsu High, preferring to travel the world to find a way to eliminate cursed energy entirely.
Her innate technique, Star Rage, allows her to add an overwhelming amount of "virtual mass" to herself and her shikigami, Garuda.

Geto is the love-to-hate guy. At one time, best buddies with Gojo Satoru at Jujutsu High, he turned against the human world after hating humans for literally existing. His main thing? Collecting cursed spirits like some twisted Pokémon trainer.

Popular Curses

Dangerous cursed spirits in the JJK universe.

Sukuna is the most powerful Special Grade Curse and is the undisputed King of Curses in Jujutsu Kaisen.
He used to be an undefeated sorcerer as a human and felled countless other sorcerers. His innate technique, Shrine, allows him to launch slashes to cut through objects and space. He can use Dismantle, Cleave, and Spiderweb.
His Domain Expansion, Malevolent Shrine, allows everything within it to be relentlessly slashed.

Rika is the Cursed Spirit that remains with Yuta. Rika Orimoto, Yuta’s childhood friend, died in an accident and transformed into a Special Grade vengeful Cursed Spirit.
Rika has been dubbed the Queen of Curses and is a powerful Special Grade curse with immeasurable cursed energy.

Jogo is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit and was born of humanity’s fear of earth, specifically fire-based natural disasters.
His maximum technique allows him to generate a huge flaming meteor which he hurls at his target.

Mahito is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit. His ultimate goal is the eradication of humanity and to replace the population with Cursed Spirits.
His cursed technique, Idle Transfiguration, lets him reshape souls.

Hanami is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit who was born from the fear of land-based natural disasters.
They can control plants and have an uncanny ability to hide their presence.

Dagon is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit who was born from humanity’s fear of water-based natural disasters (controlling large bodies of H2O).
His Domain Expansion creates a sunny tropical island where his shikigami can bite his prey.

Choso is a much more powerful Special Grade Curse than his brothers Eso and Kechizu.
He is a Cursed Womb: Death Painting who is a master at hand-to-hand combat, utilizing Blood Manipulation.

Major Fights Throughout the Series

An overview of the most iconic clashes in the JJK universe.

Season 1: The Beginning
Yuji & Todo vs. Hanami: A showcase of the Boogie Woogie technique and Yuji landing his 1st consecutive Black Flashes.
Kokichi Muta & Mechamaru vs. Mahito: A battle of wits and technology against a shape-shifting curse, showcasing Mechamaru's strategic prowess.
Yuji & Nobara vs. Eso & Kechizu: A brutal fight demonstrating the Resonance technique and Yuji's poison resistance. Gojo vs. Jogo: The overwhelming display of Gojo's Limitless techniques and Domain Expansion, leaving Jogo completely outclassed. Yuji & Nanami vs Mahito:Yuji Itadori & Kento Nanami vs. Mahito is a battle fought between jujutsu sorcerers Yuji Itadori and Kento Nanami against the special grade cursed spirit Mahito outside Satozakura High.
Season 2: Hidden Inventory & Shibuya Incident
Satoru Gojo vs. Toji Fushiguro: The awakening of the strongest sorcerer and the mastering of Reverse Cursed Technique.
Satoru Gojo vs. Mahito Group: Satoru Gojo exorcises Hanami and a thousand Transfigured Humans. Jogo, Choso, and Mahito buy enough time for Pseudo-Geto to activate Prison Realm and seal Satoru.
Jujutsu Sorcerers vs. Dagon: Naobito initially overpowers Dagon until he traps all his adversaries inside Horizon of the Captivating Skandha. After Megumi arrives late to help,
Toji is accidentally allowed inside the domain and takes over the fight. Toji overpowers Dagon and easily exorcises the special grade cursed spirit.
Sukuna vs. Jogo:Sukuna defeats Jogo without suffering a single hit. He admits Jogo is strong and exorcises him in a blaze of glory.
Ryomen Sukuna vs. Mahoraga: A devastating display of power using Malevolent Shrine and fire manipulation to destroy the Divine General.
Yuji Itadori & Aoi Todo vs. Mahito: The grueling climax of the Shibuya Incident involving a 120% potential awakening and the true shape of the soul.
Season 3:The Culling Game Arc(2026)
Yuta Okkotsu vs. Sendai Colony: Yuta taking on multiple special grade level opponents (Uro, Ryu, and Kurourushi) simultaneously.
Choso vs. Naoya Zenin: Choso defeats Naoya but is incapacitated by Yuta shortly after.
Maki vs. Zenin Clan: Maki takes on the entire Zenin Clan, showcasing her incredible physical prowess and strategic mind, ultimately leading to the downfall of the clan's leadership.
After Mai sacrifices her life to unbind her twin from cursed energy, Maki easily beheads her father.
Maki easily overpowers and slaughters the entire unit, bar the captain, before the Hei arrive to face her.
Maki eliminates Jinichi, Ranta, Chojuro, and Nobuaki.
Maki figures out how Naoya's technique works and defeats him with a single punch.
Yuji vs. Higuruma: Higuruma allows himself to be defeated instead of executing an innocent man. He uses his 100 points to add a rule to the Culling Game for Yuji.
Megumi Fushiguro vs. Reggie Star: Megumi is victorious and Reggie transfers all 41 of his points to him.
