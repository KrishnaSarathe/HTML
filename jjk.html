Welcome to Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese anime and manga series that follows Yuji Itadori as he battles curses with the help of his friends and mentors. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and dark themes. The story revolves around Yuji Itadori, a high school student with exceptional physical abilities. After the death of his grandfather, Yuji finds himself drawn into the world of Jujutsu Sorcery when he comes into contact with a cursed object—a rotting finger imbued with an ancient curse.

Strongest Sorcerers

    • Our very own Yuji is now all grown up (too much, actually), and in Volume 3 of Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo (via @Go_Jover), he has finally been revealed as a special grade sorcerer.
    He is no longer the neglected main character that many claimed he was; he is now the face of the franchise, the strongest in the verse. If you thought Gojo was overpowered, wait until you learn about Yuji’s current abilities. Drastically slowed down aging, Blood Manipulation, Sukuna’s techniques, RCT, Black Flash, Domain Expansion, immense strength and speed; you name it, Yuji has it.
    His growth after the final battle against Sukuna has been unprecedented over the years, and it was well demonstrated against the rematch against Mahito (if you wanna call it that even) in Modulo Chapter 22.

    • Satoru Gojo is a Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest sorcerer in the world. He is the pride of the Gojo Family, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years.
    He works as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High and uses his influence to protect and train strong young allies.
    His Domain Expansion, Unlimited Void, overwhelms the target's mind with endless information, leaving them completely paralyzed. In Jujutsu Kaisen, Gojo isn’t only breaking the game; he IS THE GAME. With Limitless and the Six Eyes, he can perceive and manipulate everything while hardly using any of his cursed energy.
    His Infinity makes attacks stop before they even reach him, and his Red, Blue, and Purple skills can wipe matter from existence like it never existed. He also mixes enhanced martial arts to slap around special-grade cursed spirits like they’re training dummies.
    Gojo is literally the ceiling of power in the Jujutsu universe, and nobody comes close. He’s the guy every cursed spirit secretly fears, and every fan secretly wishes they could fight, or perhaps not.

    • Yuta Okkotsu is a Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerer who was initially cursed by his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto. After enrolling in Jujutsu High, he learned to channel her immense power.
    He possesses even more cursed energy than Satoru Gojo and has the rare ability to unconditionally copy other cursed techniques.
    He is a prodigy in combat and is widely considered to be second only to Satoru Gojo in modern sorcery. Yuta’s story hits different in Jujutsu Kaisen. Once a cursed human haunted by his childhood friend Rika (turned into a curse), he learned to harness that power and became one of the top dogs. Rika’s cursed energy is insane, and Yuta can tap into it like it’s nothing, boosting his attacks and healing himself like a pro. Yuta Okkotsu channeling Rika’s immense cursed energy while wielding his sword. He’s also a tech wizard in cursed techniques, copying Toge Inumaki’s Cursed Speech, summoning shikigami, and more. On top of that, his swordplay is top-tier, making him deadly in every type of fight. If you thought Geto and Yuki were scary, Yuta’s versatility and raw power put him a way higher.

    • Kenjaku is an ancient and powerful curse user in Jujutsu Kaisen who extends his life by transplanting his brain into other bodies, manipulating events over centuries to achieve his goals. Identity and Background Kenjaku is a centuries-old sorcerer whose true appearance is unknown. He has survived for over a thousand years by using his innate technique, Brain Transplant, which allows him to transfer his brain into other bodies and control them while retaining his own consciousness and memories. Over time, he has inhabited numerous individuals, including Noritoshi Kamo, the creator of the Cursed Womb: Death Paintings, Kaori Itadori, Yuji Itadori’s mother, and Suguru Geto, whose body he used to exploit Cursed Spirit Manipulation.
    Each host retains Kenjaku’s signature stitched scar on the forehead, marking the brain transplant site. Personality Kenjaku is manipulative, egotistical, and highly intelligent. He can appear cheerful, playful, or charming, but beneath this demeanor lies a calculating and ruthless individual who will harm anyone to achieve his ambitions. He enjoys long monologues, often educating or taunting others while demonstrating his superiority. Motivations Kenjaku’s ultimate goal is to push human evolution to the next stage by merging humanity with Tengen and manipulating cursed energy on a global scale. His actions are not merely for destruction; they are part of a long-term vision to reshape the world according to his designs. He has been influencing Jujutsu society for centuries, using his hosts to manipulate sorcerers and orchestrate major events. Summary Kenjaku is the central antagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen, embodying centuries of cunning, power, and manipulation. Through his brain-transplant technique, mastery of cursed techniques, and strategic foresight, he has become a nearly unstoppable force, shaping the series’ events and challenging the protagonists in profound ways. Abilities and Skills Kenjaku is an exceptionally skilled sorcerer with a deep understanding of jujutsu techniques. His abilities include: Cursed Spirit Manipulation: Mastered while inhabiting Suguru Geto’s body, allowing him to control powerful cursed spirits. Barrier Techniques: Recognized as the second most powerful barrier user after Tengen. Turning Sorcerers into Cursed Objects: A rare technique even Sukuna did not know during the Heian Era. Brain Transplant: Enables him to inherit the abilities, cursed energy, and memories of his hosts, effectively making him adaptable and nearly immortal. Kenjaku is also a master strategist, often planning events centuries in advance, such as orchestrating the Shibuya Incident and initiating the Culling Game.

    • Yuki Tsukumo is one of the four Special Grade Jujutsu Sorcerers. Unlike the others, she does not take on missions from Jujutsu High, preferring to travel the world to find a way to eliminate cursed energy entirely.
    Her innate technique, Star Rage, allows her to add an overwhelming amount of "virtual mass" to herself and her shikigami, Garuda.
    This makes her physical strikes devastatingly powerful, capable of shattering even the strongest defenses. Yuki is one of the rarest special grades, and she likes keeping it that way. She doesn’t care much for missions unless she wants to, and her philosophy is 'stop curses before they even exist.' Sounds chill, but also terrifying if you’re a curse. She trained Aoi Todo, teaching him insane stuff like Simple Domain counters against Domain Expansions. Yuki Tsukumo unleashing her Star Rage technique with Garuda by her side. In battle, Yuki is all about throwing hard and fast. Her cursed technique, Star Rage, provides her and her shikigami Garuda with virtual mass, so her blows strike like a truck, or a building, probably. Add her Simple Domain that cancels out other cursed techniques, and she’s basically unstoppable up close. We haven’t seen her at full strength yet, but the rumors are terrifying enough.

    • Geto is the love-to-hate guy. At one time, best buddies with Gojo Satoru at Jujutsu High, he turned against the human world after hating humans for literally existing. His main thing? Collecting cursed spirits like some twisted Pokémon trainer. Each curse has unique abilities, and he can combine them for some devastating attacks. Geto also has crazy reflexes, battle IQ, and can swing the Playful Cloud (a three-section staff that’s deadly in his hands). Back in his student days, he was basically Gojo’s equal, but now? He’s strong, scary, but not quite top-tier anymore.

Popular Curses

    • Sukuna is the most powerful Special Grade Curse and is the undisputed King of Curses in Jujutsu Kaisen.
    He used to be an undefeated sorcerer as a human and felled countless other sorcerers. Even after death, he continued existing as a cursed object by dividing his cursed energy and soul into his indestructible fingers. Sukuna has an immense amount of cursed energy and is a master at hand-to-hand combat. He also has unmatched physical strength, speed, reflexes, and durability.
    Needless to say, he is the biggest antagonist of the story. He is a sadist to the core and also equipped with great tactical intellect. His innate technique, Shrine, allows him to launch slashes to cut through objects and space. He can use Dismantle, Cleave, and Spiderweb, and can gain access to tons of Shikigami.
    His most powerful shinigami is Mahoraga. He is also able to completely heal himself via Reverse Cursed Technique. His Domain Expansion, Malevolent Shrine, allows everything within it to be relentlessly slashed.

    • Rika is the Cursed Spirit that remains with Yuta. Rika Orimoto, Yuta’s childhood friend, died in an accident and transformed into a Special Grade vengeful Cursed Spirit.
    She remained with Yuta for decades till he allowed her to pass on, leaving a Cursed Spirit as the last vestiges of her will. Rika has been dubbed the Queen of Curses and is a powerful Special Grade curse with immesuarable cursed energy. She can use both physical attacks and jujutsu, and even tore apart a huge spirit with her bare hands.
    Rika can copy techniques, even the Inumaki Family’s technique, reinforcing commands with compulsion. Yuta can access these techniques via Rika. She can also rip open her chest to expose an entire arsenal of cursed tools stored within her body for Yuta’s access.

    • Jogo is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit and was born of humanity’s fear of earth, specifically fire-based natural disasters.
    Even Sukuna acknowledged that Jogo’s maximum technique would have hurt him had it landed. His cursed energy can be comparable to 8 or 9 of Sukuna’s fingers. His innate volcanic technique allows him to produce cursed flames or manifest small volcanic openings. His maximum technique allows him to generate a huge flaming meteor which he hurls a his target.
    His Domain Expansion, Coffing of the Iron Mountain, constructs the inside of a volcano, which he can manipulate at will. An average sorcerer burns up on impact, and it’s a deadly attack.

    • Mahito is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit. His ultimate goal is the eradication of humanity and to replace the population with Cursed Spirits.
    His power exceeded that of at least three of Sukuna’s fingers when he first encountered Yuji. He has a rapid growth rate and high intelligence, which render him dangerous. He was capable of overwhelming Namami and Yuji, if not for Sukuna’s intervention. He has a vast amount of cursed energy, and he uses Black Flash, a potent power.
    His cursed technique lets him reshape souls, and he uses it to disfigure the bodies of his victims, heal his own wounds, and for other uses. He can also merge souls together or strengthen his own body.
    Mahito can create a barrier called a Curtain, and his Domain Expansion, Self Embodiment of Perfection, lets him create an environment where his Idle Transfiguration Technique becomes guaranteed to hit his targets.

    • Hanami is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit who was born from the fear of land-based natural disasters.
    They can control plants and have an uncanny ability to hide their presence. They were even able to take Satoru Gojo by surprise due to their high intellect and escape abilities. Hanami passed through the barriers of Jujutsu High and took on six of their elite students. Hanami uses Disaster Plants to manifest illusions of cursed plants into reality.
    Some of his most powerful techniques include creating wooden balls from cursed energy, which can be used to skewer targets using cursed buds, which can feed on the cursed energy of those it attaches to, and creating a circle of flowers, which can take away the surrounded person’s desire to fight. Their Domain Expansion, Shining Sea of Growing Branches, creates a beam that is charged with the curse’s left arm.

    • Dagon is an unregistered Special Grade Cursed Spirit who was born from humanity’s fear of water-based natural disasters.
    Dagon’s evolved state is extremely powerful due to his heightened intelligence and enhanced physical form. Dagon possesses massive amounts of cursed energy and a multitude of cursed techniques. His innate technique, Disaster Tides, lets him produce massive waves of water without any source except simple cursed energy.
    He can also release tidal waves from his fingertips and drown his opponents. He can conjure Shikigami, which vary from man-eating fish, sea-serpents, to other dangerous marine life, which he can organize into a death swarm. His Domain Expansion creates a sunny tropical island where his shinigami can bite his prey.

    • Choso is a much more powerful Special Grade Curse than his brothers Eso and Kechizu.
    He is a Cursed Womb: Death Painting who is a master at hand-to-hand combat, which, combined with his tactical intelligence, strength, and endurance, makes him a daunting opponent. Like Eso and Kechizu, he can also use Rot and Decay with his poisoned blood, but he has mastered blood manipulation better than his brothers. He can use sharpened blood, which can slice through his targets before infecting them. He also developed Supernova, which detonates orbs of blood in every direction.
    He can improve his own performance by pumping blood faster throughout his body.
